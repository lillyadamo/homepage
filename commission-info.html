commission information

I'm so glad you're here! I promise that I will deliver you the best piece I can create, and I will not settle for anything less!
The mediums I work in are: Charcoal, Oil and Acryilc, and digital!

Pricing commissions is a complex endeavour. I'll explain how I price my pieces so you can have a better idea on what you can expect to pay!

For more simplistic pieces, I use the equation: time + materials = commission price.
Prices will increase/decrease depending on what medium the piece is completed in.
An example of a less complex piece would be: a portrait from the chest up with a plain background.
I would also price most digital art under this category.

For more complex piece, I use the equation: $n × (Years’ Experience) × (Difficulty Multiplier) + Materials = Commission Price (where n= the number of years I've been taking commissions)
I prefer to use this for more complex pieces as it would most likely take me a while to complete and therefor would not want to charge an hourly rate.
An example of a more complex piece would be: an oil painting on a canvas with single or multiple individuals and multiple complex elements.

If this sounds good to you and you're interested in commissioning some art from me, please email me and we can discuss further!
If you aren't schedulling a meeting and are okay with doing everything over email, please have some reference photos ready for me (if they're needed!)
